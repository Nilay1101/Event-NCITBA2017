About Us

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering has been set up to impart training of the highest standards to the students, in the field of computers, thus preparing them to meet the exacting demands of the highly competitive global industrial market.
The Department is currently focused on providing B.Tech., M.Tech., M.C.A. & PhD programs which cover exhaustively the area of Computer Science & Engineering:.

Objective

    The National Conference on Information Technology and Business Analytics - 2017 (NCITBA-2017) aims to provide an outstanding opportunity for both academic and industrial communities alike to address new trends and challenges and emerging technologies on topics relevant to today’s fast moving areas of Information Technology & Informatics. The vision of NCITBA-2017 is to develop foster communication among researchers and practitioners working in a wide variety of areas in Information Technology & Informatics with a common interest.

paper Submission Guidelines

We solicit original, unpublished, and innovative research work on applying any intelligent methods to all aspects around the theme of this conference.
  1. The papers should not exceed 6 A-4 two-column pages and not shorter than 4 pages of technical content including figures and references. Refer : IEEE format: Latex / Word
  2. Papers having similarity measure of more than 20% will not be accepted for review. Refer: Plagiarism
  3. At least one author should be registered in order to have their paper accepted for the conference. The corresponding author will be solely responsible for the content of the submitted paper(s).
  4. All submitted papers will be sent to reviewers for a blind review. The parameters based on which reviewers will evaluate the papers are Technical Content and Scientific Rigour, Novelty and Originality, Quality of Presentation, Relevance and Timeliness.
  5. All selected and presented papers will be part of Conference Proceedings ans the selected papers will be published in International Journal of Communication Networks and Information Security (IJCNIS).
  6. All submissions must be made online within the published deadlines on the conference website.

paper Selection process

Step 1 : (Received) Abstracts* / full length papers received will be given paper ID in relevant to the conference topic & track.
Step 2 : (Under Screening) Initial screening review will be done by the Technical Program Committee of NCITBA-2017. Initial screening include relevance to the conference topic, plagiarism***, organization of the paper, quality of figures/diagrams/illustrations/equations and etc.
Step 3 : x-fast(Under Review) Papers got through Step 2 alone will be forwarded to technical review process.
Step 4 : (Revision) Corresponding author will be notified the outcome of the review process and the revised version of the manuscript shall be submitted within five days on the date of intimation.
Step 5 : (Under Review) Revised version of the papers will be sent for reviewer to ensure that all the suggested corrections have been carried out. If the reviewer recommended for minor revision TPC recommends its decision to the organizing Chair on the paper.
Step 6 : (Accept / Reject) Final decision will be taken by the organizing chair based on the recommendations by the reviewers / TPC and the same will be intimated to the corresponding authors.
Step 7 : (CRP Received / Not Received) status of the Camera Ready Paper after the acceptance.
Step 8 : (REG Complete / Pending) Status of the registration
* No paper will be accepted without screening review and technical review process
** Full length papers shall be submitted in order to consider the paper for review process.
*** The authors must check the possible plagiarism in the paper before submission
